Frequently Asked Questions

🪙
My coins disappeared after reinstalling the app
+

We're sorry to hear that! Unfortunately, all in-game data in Towerific — including your coin balance, high score, level, and streak — is stored locally on your iPhone. This means it is not backed up to any cloud service or server.

When you uninstall and reinstall the app, the local data is permanently deleted by iOS as part of the uninstall process. We have no way to recover it on our end.

Tip: To avoid losing your coins, make sure to keep the app installed and avoid uninstalling unless necessary. If you purchased coins through the App Store, please see the FAQ below about restoring purchases.
🛒
How do I restore my purchases?
+

If you've previously purchased coin packs and want to restore them on a new device or after reinstalling, you can attempt a restore through the in-game settings:

  1. Open Towerific on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon) on the main menu screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Restore Purchases.
  4. A prompt from Apple may appear asking you to sign in to your Apple ID — follow the on-screen instructions.

Please note that Towerific's coin packs are consumable items. This means they are one-time-use purchases. Once coins have been spent in-game, they cannot be restored via this method — only unspent/unused coin packs may be eligible for restoration, depending on Apple's policies.

If you have further issues with purchases, please contact Apple directly at reportaproblem.apple.com.

📺
An ad is not working or the screen is frozen
+

Occasionally, an advertisement may fail to load, become unresponsive, or cause the screen to appear frozen. This is usually caused by a temporary network issue or an ad loading error from the ad provider (Google AdMob).

Here's what to try:

  • Close and reopen the app: Double-press the Home button (or swipe up and hold on Face ID iPhones) to see open apps, then swipe up on Towerific to close it. Relaunch the app from your home screen.
  • Check your internet connection: Ads require an active internet connection to load. Make sure you are connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data.
  • Restart your iPhone: If the issue persists, restarting your device often resolves temporary glitches.

If you continue to experience frozen ads regularly, please contact us with details about your device model and iOS version — we'll report it to Google AdMob.

🚫
How do I turn off ads?
+

Advertisements are how we keep Towerific free to play for everyone. Without ad revenue, we would not be able to maintain and improve the game.

In the current version (v1) of Towerific, there is no option to remove ads — either as a separate purchase or as part of any coin pack. Ads will appear between game sessions as part of the standard gameplay experience.

Future update: We are considering a "Remove Ads" option in a future update. Stay tuned to our App Store page for news on upcoming features.

You can control whether ads are personalised by managing your tracking settings. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and toggle Towerific on or off. This does not remove ads but controls whether they are tailored to your interests.

What are power-ups and how do I use them?
+

Power-ups are special in-game boosters that can give you an advantage during gameplay. They are purchased using coins — which you can earn by playing or buy through in-app purchases.

Power-ups available in Towerific include helpful tools to extend your tower, correct misaligned blocks, or gain extra chances when the game would otherwise end. Specific power-up details and effects are shown within the game UI before purchase.

How to use a power-up:

  1. During a game session, look for the power-up tray on the game screen.
  2. Tap a power-up icon to activate it (it will deduct the coin cost).
  3. The power-up effect is applied immediately to your current game.

Power-ups are consumable — each use deducts from your coin balance. They do not carry over between sessions once used.

🔥
How does the daily streak work?
+

The daily streak tracks how many consecutive days you have opened and played Towerific. It rewards you for building a consistent play habit!

How it works:

  • Open the game and play at least one round each day to keep your streak alive.
  • Each new day you play, your streak counter increases by 1.
  • If you miss a day (i.e., you do not play within a 24-hour window), your streak resets to zero.
  • Longer streaks unlock bigger daily rewards, including bonus coins.
Tip: The streak resets based on calendar days. Try to log in at roughly the same time each day to make sure you don't accidentally miss a day.

Streak progress is saved locally on your device. If you reinstall the app, your streak will be lost along with other local data — see the coins FAQ above for more details.
