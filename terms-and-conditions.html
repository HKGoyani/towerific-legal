2 About the Game

Towerific — Tower Stack Rush is a hyper-casual, free-to-play mobile game available exclusively on iOS for iPhone via the Apple App Store. The objective of the Game is to stack coloured blocks as high as possible to build a tower, testing your timing and precision.

Key features of the Game include:

  • Endless hyper-casual tower stacking gameplay
  • A daily streak and reward system to encourage regular play
  • An XP and levelling system
  • In-game coins earned through play or purchasable via the App Store

The Game is available free of charge. Optional in-app purchases are available to enhance your experience. No purchase is required to enjoy the core gameplay.
