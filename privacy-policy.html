Privacy Policy

App: Towerific — Tower Stack Rush  ·  Developer: Shayona  ·  Last updated: May 28, 2026

1 Overview

Towerific — Tower Stack Rush ("the Game", "we", "our", or "us") is a free-to-play mobile game developed by Shayona and published on the Apple App Store for iPhone. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is and is not collected when you play the Game, how advertising is served, and how your in-app purchases are handled. By downloading and playing the Game, you agree to this policy.

In short: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). All gameplay data is stored only on your device. Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) with your permission.

2 Information We Do Not Collect

Towerific does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on our servers:

  • Your name, username, or any identifier you choose
  • Your email address or phone number
  • Your precise or approximate location (GPS, Wi-Fi triangulation, etc.)
  • Access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or photos
  • Your device's calendar, reminders, or health data
  • Your browsing history or search history
  • Any financial or payment information
  • Your Apple ID or any third-party account credentials
  • Your IP address or persistent device identifiers (beyond what AdMob collects — see Section 4)

We have no backend servers that receive gameplay data. Your experience is entirely local to your device.

3 Data Stored on Your Device

The Game saves certain gameplay information locally on your iPhone using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults / local file system). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us. It includes:

  • High score — your best tower height or score achieved
  • Coins — your current in-game coin balance earned or purchased
  • Settings — sound on/off, haptics preferences, and other display settings
  • Daily streak — how many consecutive days you have played
  • XP and level — your current experience points and player level
  • Remove Ads status — whether you have purchased the "Remove Ads" non-consumable IAP (stored as a local flag so the entitlement persists across app restarts)

This data is deleted automatically when you uninstall the Game. We have no ability to recover it once deleted.

4 Advertising — Google AdMob

The Game is free to play and is supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob (a service provided by Google LLC) to serve advertisements within the Game.

What AdMob may collect: Google AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA — Identifier for Advertisers), as well as general device and usage information, to serve ads that may be relevant to you. This is only done after you grant permission through the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt (see Section 5).

Personalised vs. non-personalised ads:

  • If you allow tracking in the ATT prompt, AdMob may show you personalised ads based on your interests and prior app activity across other companies' apps and websites.
  • If you decline tracking, AdMob will only show contextual (non-personalised) ads and your IDFA will not be used for targeted advertising.

Google's data practices are governed by their own privacy policy. You can review it at:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ad Settings & Opt-Out (adssettings.google.com)

We do not control the content of ads served by AdMob and are not responsible for the data practices of Google or its advertising partners.

Remove Ads purchase: Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will no longer see banner, interstitial, or app-open advertisements. Rewarded ads (opt-in video ads the player voluntarily watches to earn free coins) remain available regardless of this purchase, as they are a player benefit rather than an interruption. When ads are removed, AdMob is simply not called for those ad types — no additional data is collected.

5 App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework (available on iOS 14.5 and later), the Game will present you with a system prompt the first time you launch it. This prompt asks whether you consent to the Game tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites.

If you tap "Allow": Your IDFA is shared with Google AdMob for the purpose of showing you personalised advertisements. This may result in ads that are more relevant to your interests.

If you tap "Ask App Not to Track": Your IDFA is not shared with AdMob. You will still see ads, but they will be non-personalised (contextual only). The Game's core functionality remains fully available regardless of your choice.

Changing your choice later: You can change your tracking preference at any time by going to:

  • Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, then toggle Towerific on or off.
  • Alternatively, go to Settings → Towerific and adjust the tracking permission there.
Your choice does not affect your ability to play the Game. All features, including in-app purchases and gameplay, work identically regardless of your ATT decision.

6 In-App Purchases

Towerific offers the following in-app purchases, processed exclusively through Apple App Store:

Consumable coin packs (×5): Optional coin packs at five price points ($0.99 – $14.99). Coins are an in-game virtual currency earned through gameplay or purchased as an optional shortcut. Consumable purchases are spent and cannot be restored once used.

Non-consumable — Remove Ads ($2.99): A one-time purchase that permanently removes banner, interstitial, and app-open advertisements. Rewarded ads (opt-in) remain available. As a non-consumable purchase, this entitlement can be restored on a new device or after reinstallation using the Restore Purchases button available on the Get Coins screen.

Payment processing: All in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple App Store using Apple StoreKit. We, Shayona, never receive, process, or store your payment card details, Apple ID credentials, or any other financial information. Apple's payment policies and data practices apply to all transactions.

Receipts and verification: Apple provides us only with transaction receipt data (purchase confirmation and product ID) to verify that a purchase was made. No personal or financial details are shared with us.

Refunds: Refund requests must be submitted to Apple. You can request a refund through Apple's report-a-problem page at reportaproblem.apple.com.

8 Children's Privacy

Towerific is a casual tower-stacking game suitable for players of all ages. The Game is rated suitable for all audiences on the Apple App Store.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because we collect no personal data at all, the Game is safe for children to play.

However, the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt relates to ad personalisation. For children under 13, parents or guardians should make the tracking consent decision. You can disable tracking for all apps on a device by going to Settings → Screen Time → Content & Privacy Restrictions → Tracking.

If you believe a child has been subject to advertising practices that are inappropriate, please contact us using the details in Section 12 and we will investigate.

9 Data Security

Because Towerific does not transmit any gameplay data to external servers, the primary security consideration is the protection of your device's local storage, which is managed by Apple's iOS security model (sandboxing, data protection APIs).

For advertising data, Google AdMob communicates with Google's servers over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections. We do not have control over these communications, but Google employs industry-standard security practices.

For in-app purchases, all payment data is handled exclusively by Apple's secure payment infrastructure. We never receive or store payment credentials.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. However, given that we collect no personal data, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal information through our Game is minimal.

10 Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, many traditional data subject rights (access, rectification, portability) are not directly applicable. However, you have the following controls:

  • Opt out of personalised ads: Decline the ATT prompt, or change your tracking settings in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Reset advertising ID: On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track". This limits ad tracking for all apps.
  • Opt out via Google: Visit adssettings.google.com to manage your Google ad preferences.
  • Delete your game data: Uninstalling the Game permanently deletes all locally stored data (scores, coins, settings, streak, XP). This action cannot be undone.
  • Contact us: If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, contact us at the address in Section 12.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. Contact us to exercise those rights.
