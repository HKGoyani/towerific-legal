Privacy Policy

App: Towerific  ·  Developer: Shayona  ·  Platforms: iOS & Android  ·  Last updated: June 16, 2026

1 Overview

Towerific ("the Game", "we", "our", or "us") is a free-to-play mobile game developed by Shayona, available on the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is and is not collected when you play the Game, how advertising is served, and how your in-app purchases are handled. It applies equally to both the iOS and Android versions. By downloading and playing the Game, you agree to this policy.

In short: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). All gameplay data is stored only on your device. Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android) subject to your consent.

2 Information We Do Not Collect

Towerific does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on our servers:

  • Your name, username, or any identifier you choose
  • Your email address or phone number
  • Your precise or approximate location (GPS, Wi-Fi triangulation, etc.)
  • Access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or photos
  • Your device's calendar, reminders, or health data
  • Your browsing history or search history
  • Any financial or payment information
  • Your Apple ID or any third-party account credentials
  • Your IP address or persistent device identifiers (beyond what AdMob collects — see Section 4)

We have no backend servers that receive gameplay data. Your experience is entirely local to your device.

3 Data Stored on Your Device

The Game saves certain gameplay information locally on your device using platform-standard storage (UserDefaults / local file system on iOS; SharedPreferences / local file system on Android). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us. It includes:

  • High score — your best tower height or score achieved
  • Coins — your current in-game coin balance earned or purchased
  • Settings — sound on/off, haptics preferences, and other display settings
  • Daily streak — how many consecutive days you have played
  • XP and level — your current experience points and player level
  • Remove Ads status — whether you have purchased the "Remove Ads" non-consumable IAP (stored as a local flag so the entitlement persists across app restarts)
  • Notification preference — whether you've enabled local reminder notifications (see Section 6)

This data is deleted automatically when you uninstall the Game. We have no ability to recover it once deleted.

4 Advertising — Google AdMob

The Game is free to play and is supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob (a service provided by Google LLC) to serve advertisements within the Game.

What AdMob may collect: Google AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS / Google Advertising ID on Android), as well as general device and usage information, to serve ads that may be relevant to you. On iOS this requires your consent via the App Tracking Transparency prompt (see Section 5). On Android, you can opt out via your device's ad settings (see Section 5).

Personalised vs. non-personalised ads:

  • If you allow tracking (iOS) or have not opted out of ad personalisation (Android), AdMob may show you personalised ads based on your interests and prior app activity.
  • If you decline tracking (iOS) or opt out of personalised ads (Android), AdMob will only show contextual (non-personalised) ads and your advertising identifier will not be used for targeted advertising.

Google's data practices are governed by their own privacy policy. You can review it at:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ad Settings & Opt-Out (adssettings.google.com)

We do not control the content of ads served by AdMob and are not responsible for the data practices of Google or its advertising partners.

Remove Ads purchase: Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will no longer see banner, interstitial, or app-open advertisements. Rewarded ads (opt-in video ads the player voluntarily watches to earn free coins) remain available regardless of this purchase, as they are a player benefit rather than an interruption. When ads are removed, AdMob is simply not called for those ad types — no additional data is collected.

5 Ad Tracking & Consent

iOS — App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework (iOS 14.5+), the Game may present you with a system prompt asking whether you consent to tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites.

  • Allow: Your IDFA is shared with Google AdMob for personalised ads.
  • Ask App Not to Track: Your IDFA is not shared. Ads remain but are non-personalised (contextual only).

Change your choice anytime: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Towerific.

Android — Google Advertising ID

On Android, Google AdMob may use your Google Advertising ID (GAID) to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of ad personalisation at any time:

  • Android 12+: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or opt out of personalised ads).
  • Android 11 and below: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
Your tracking or ad personalisation choice does not affect your ability to play the Game. All features, including in-app purchases and gameplay, work identically regardless of your decision.

6 Notifications

Towerific can send you local reminder notifications — for example, a nudge to keep your daily streak alive or a heads-up that the weekly leaderboard has reset. The Game will request notification permission via the standard iOS system prompt after you've played a few rounds.

These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. We do not operate a push-notification server, and no data about you or your gameplay is sent anywhere to schedule or deliver them.

On Android (13+), the Game will request the POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission the first time you enable reminders. You can grant or revoke this at any time in your device settings.

Managing notifications:

  • Toggle reminders on or off anytime in Towerific → Settings → Notifications.
  • iOS: Go to Settings → Towerific → Notifications to change or revoke permission.
  • Android: Go to Settings → Apps → Towerific → Notifications to change or revoke permission.
Notifications are entirely optional. Declining or disabling them does not affect your ability to play the Game or access any feature.

7 In-App Purchases

Towerific offers the following in-app purchases, processed by the platform store (Apple App Store on iOS; Google Play Store on Android):

Consumable coin packs (×6): Optional coin packs at six price points ($0.99 – $14.99), including a one-time "Welcome Offer" (2,000 coins for $1.99) shown to new players for 24 hours after their first launch. Coins are an in-game virtual currency earned through gameplay or purchased as an optional shortcut. Consumable purchases are spent and cannot be restored once used.

Non-consumable — Remove Ads ($2.99): A one-time purchase that permanently removes banner, interstitial, and app-open advertisements. Rewarded ads (opt-in) remain available. As a non-consumable purchase, this entitlement can be restored on a new device or after reinstallation using the Restore Purchases button available on the Get Coins screen.

Payment processing:

  • iOS: All purchases are processed entirely by Apple App Store using Apple StoreKit. Apple's payment policies and data practices apply.
  • Android: All purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. Google's payment policies and data practices apply.

We, Shayona, never receive, process, or store your payment card details, Apple ID / Google account credentials, or any other financial information.

Refunds:

  • iOS: Submit refund requests to Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
  • Android: Submit refund requests through Google Play at play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory.

9 Children's Privacy

Towerific is a casual tower-stacking game suitable for players of all ages. The Game is rated suitable for all audiences on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because we collect no personal data at all, the Game is safe for children to play.

However, ad personalisation prompts relate to advertising identifiers. For children under 13, parents or guardians should manage these settings:

  • iOS: Disable tracking for all apps via Settings → Screen Time → Content & Privacy Restrictions → Tracking.
  • Android: On Android 12+, go to Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID to prevent personalised ads.

If you believe a child has been subject to advertising practices that are inappropriate, please contact us using the details in Section 13 and we will investigate.

10 Data Security

Because Towerific does not transmit any gameplay data to external servers, the primary security consideration is the protection of your device's local storage, which is managed by the platform's security model (iOS sandboxing and data protection APIs; Android app sandboxing and scoped storage).

For advertising data, Google AdMob communicates with Google's servers over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections. We do not have control over these communications, but Google employs industry-standard security practices.

For in-app purchases, all payment data is handled exclusively by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android) secure payment infrastructure. We never receive or store payment credentials.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. However, given that we collect no personal data, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal information through our Game is minimal.

11 Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, many traditional data subject rights (access, rectification, portability) are not directly applicable. However, you have the following controls:

  • Opt out of personalised ads (iOS): Decline the ATT prompt, or go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Towerific to change your choice at any time.
  • Opt out of personalised ads (Android): Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads (Android 12+) or Settings → Google → Ads (Android 11 and below) and select "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" or "Delete advertising ID".
  • Opt out via Google: Visit adssettings.google.com to manage your Google ad preferences across platforms.
  • Delete your game data: Uninstalling the Game permanently deletes all locally stored data (scores, coins, settings, streak, XP). This action cannot be undone.
  • Contact us: If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, contact us at the address in Section 13.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. Contact us to exercise those rights.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. If you continue to use the Game after changes are posted, you are deemed to have accepted the updated policy.

Significant changes will be highlighted in the App Store release notes for the relevant update version.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
